Lasagna

Description

The lasagna is one of the world's loved foods!

Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Minced Meat
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Lasagna Sheets

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Layer meat sauce on base
  3. Repeat layers and cover with lasagna sheets
  4. Sprinkle top with cheese
  5. Pop in the oven
